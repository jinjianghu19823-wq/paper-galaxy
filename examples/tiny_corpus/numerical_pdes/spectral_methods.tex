\title{Spectral Methods For Smooth PDEs}
\section{Chebyshev Differentiation}
Spectral methods approximate smooth functions using global polynomial or
trigonometric bases. Chebyshev grids, differentiation matrices, aliasing, and
boundary conditions are central tools for numerical partial differential
equations.

\section{Stability}
The notes emphasize convergence, conditioning, and how basis choice affects
derivative accuracy in elliptic and evolution equations.
