# Code of Conduct

Paper Galaxy is a small research-tool project. Please help keep the project
welcoming, careful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, research areas, and experience levels.
- Discuss ideas and code with evidence and patience.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections when something harms the community.
- Avoid posting private research data, private document text, secrets, or tokens
  in public issues.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or sustained personal attacks.
- Publishing someone else's private data without permission.
- Spam, abusive automation, or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove comments and issues that violate
this policy. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a ban from the project
spaces. If you need to report a concern, contact the maintainers through the
security or project contact channels listed in the repository.
